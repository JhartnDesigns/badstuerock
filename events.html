Cinema Squid

This project was about creating a web app that would help solve a user's problem. We focused on the issue concerning the boring and time consuming process of finding the right movie.


THE WEB APP "CINEMA SQUID"

My latest project was about the creation of a problem solving web app.

Our target audience had problems figuring out which movies to watch and was tired of searching through endless lists on streaming sites.

Our solution for this problem was making a randomizer that would grab a single movie depending on the selected movie category.

This is ultimately one of the most well recieved projects I've been a part of, and I hope to work on someting similar again.


Professor Pus

Web documentaries are an interesting medium! This was a very creative and visual project consisting of images, sound and various film clips. The final solution consisted of a web documentary for the artist Professor Pus.


WEB DOCUMENTARY FOR PROFESSOR PUS

Since this web documentary had so many visual elements, it was very important to create a balance between the visuals and the story telling.
This balance is suceeded by the use of eyecatching typography and the fact that all graphic elements had a specific role in telling the story of Professor Pus.

The visual elements are distributed between four overall categories: the still images, the videos, the patterns and of course the geometric flowers that binds it all together.

The story we had to tell was Professor Pus' musical journey. The videos tell the story of a young woman overcomming various life situations to find her voice.

Even though this project was fun and got great feedback, I have a new found appreciation for the saying: "Less is more".


Søborg Gymnasium logo

During my free time I took on a logo project. This was for one of my acquaintances that wanted a redesign of their old high school logo.

There wasn't a lot of creative thinking but the practical side of this project taught me about patience and perserverance.


LOGO FOR SØBORG GYMNASIUM

Aside from learning about patience I also learned a lot more about working in Adobe Illustrator.

As I mentioned earlier, the project didn't need any new designs. This meant that I could take my time with all the details, such as text positioning and perfect balance.

It was very refreshing to really finetune the logo before handing the finished product over to the client.


Vinyl Design - "Vincent"

One of my favorite songs at the moment is "Vincent" by Elle Goulding. I came up with the idea of making a vinyl design that would reflect the vulnerability and elegance present in the song.


VINYL DESIGN OF "VINCENT" BY ELLE GOULDING

The biggest design choice I had to make was whether or not 'Starry Night' should be on the cover or not.

I decided to put on the record instead since I really wanted to have a more subtle focal point, and although the song does mention the infamous painting, it's really not what the song is about.

The color palette for this project, was almost intuitive for me. Since there are many vibrant blues present in the painting, I wanted to create a visual contrast with a warmer tone.
